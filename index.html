Professional Experience and Academic Achievements.

2019-2025

Ph.D in Electrical and Computer Engineering

Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea

Doctoral dissertation work focuses on medical data analysis for early identification and diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease and Parkinson's disease through the application of machine learning, deep learning, information fusion, and multitasking analysis. A trustworthy clinical decision support system is the target of my research. The research employs cutting-edge technologies such as explainability, interpretability, fairness, robustness, generalization, data privacy, and security. I secured a GPA of 4.27 out of 4.5.

2014-2017

Master's Degree of Automation and Robotics

Osmania University, India

My dissertation project, “Electroencephalography (EEG) based Brain-Computer-Interface (BCI) for Controlling Real-time 2D Robot Arm,” examines the sustain and development of a disable person having abnormal motor functions to do their daily task themselves by taking signals form their brains. Built a 2R Manipulator Prototype, worked with Arduino for signals transferred, Matlab SSVEP Tool Programming and signal processing Tool, Python Programming for receive signals from brain by Emotiv EPOC device.

2010-2014

Bachelor's Degree of Mechanical Engineering

JNTU Hyderabad, India

Bachelor's degree final project entitled "Cyclic Grass Cutter with Shaft-Gear Mechanism". A shaft-gear driven cycle was assembled that is cost-effective and also drive a cutter. A grass cutter is attached to cut the leaves when shortage of power. As a team leader, worked on mechanical components designed, manufacturing them, and prepared the first draft of the thesis.
